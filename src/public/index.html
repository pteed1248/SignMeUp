About SignMeUp

The SignMeUp System serves admission, enrollment, and registration needs.

  • Student: Class schedule and registration, transcripts, official contact information
  • Faculty: Grade submission, class rosters, add/drop management, course evaluation results
  • Staff: Admissions reports, course demand, SMUGrades and SMURosters, enrollment reports, student registration
